We welcome young talents between the ages of 10 and 25. Our programs are designed to cater to different age groups, providing tailored training and mentorship to help each player grow and excel at their own pace.

Our training sessions are held twice daily — in the morning and evening. This schedule ensures that players have ample time to develop their skills, work on fitness, and engage in tactical exercises.

Yes, Kedopeach Football Academy actively participates in various local and international competitions. These competitions provide our players with the opportunity to showcase their talents, gain valuable experience, and compete at a high level, further preparing them for successful careers in football.

At Kedopeach Football Academy, we place a strong emphasis on the academic development of our players alongside their athletic training. We have structured programs that balance football training with academic education, ensuring that players attend classes regularly and meet academic requirements. We also offer mentorship and support to help players succeed both on and off the field, preparing them for a well-rounded future.
